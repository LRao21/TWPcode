About the Author

Hello! I am Lavanya (she/her). I am studying Computer Science with a minor in Mathematics at Rutgers University. After Rutgers, I want to get a master's in cybersecurity or enter the workforce directly. Wherever I end up, I hope I'll be able to go with the flow.

Hopefully, along the way, I also build some more fun things! Whenever I get a chance, I like to play Minecraft and design parts of buildings, or catch up on some old shows I love and explore new ones. Recently, I binged The Good Place and A Man on the Inside. I also played Chants of Sennaar enough times to gain every achievement and understand all of the languages throughout its plot.

I love a good narrative. Moreso, I love a good narrative about little things: the things one does not always think about, the little joys, the smaller parts of our lifestyles that can make us come together or fall apart. In details lie thought.

Computer Science is a way for me to build those narratives into something interactive and visual; I like to add some level of plot or complexity to my projects to make creating things more interactive and filling! I already plan to incorporate some of the storytelling elements and overall feel of my recent shows and gaming experiences into my projects.
